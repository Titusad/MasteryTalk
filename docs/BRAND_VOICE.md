## §2 — Voice Principles

### 1. Direct & No-Nonsense (Al grano)
We respect the user's time. In the executive world, time is money.
- **Do:** Use concise, actionable sentences.
- **Don't:** Ramble, use filler words ("Hey there!", "Just wanted to say..."), or use excessive exclamation points.

### 2. High Standards (Exigencia absoluta)
We push our users out of their comfort zone because the real market will not forgive them.
- **Do:** Highlight areas of improvement with brutal honesty but constructive framing. "Your vocabulary here sounds junior. Say 'leverage' instead of 'use'."
- **Don't:** Sugarcoat feedback. Never say "Great try!" when the performance was subpar.

### 3. Professional Empathy (Empatía ejecutiva)
We understand the struggle of competing in a non-native language, but we don't treat the user like a victim. We empower them.
- **Do:** "This phrase is costing you negotiation power. Let's fix it."
- **Don't:** "Don't worry, English is hard!" 

### 4. Zero Emojis (Estética de sistema)
Our UI and communications are clean, minimalist, and system-like.
- **Do:** Use semantic UI icons (Lucide) or clean text markers (•, -, [ ], /).
- **Don't:** Use emojis 🚀🔥🙌. We are a tool for executives, not a social network.

## §3 — Channel Execution

### 3.1 App UI & Landing Page (Spanish/Portuguese)
The interface speaks to the user in their native language to eliminate cognitive load during navigation, but relies heavily on Spanglish business terms (Sprint, Pitch, ROI, C-level).
* Example CTA: "Inicia tu sesión" or "Mejora este delivery", NOT "¡Comienza a jugar!"

### 3.2 AI Personas (Inside the Session)
Defined in `SYSTEM_PROMPTS.md`. 100% immersive English. 
* They follow the *Client*, *Manager*, or *Recruiter* psychology exactly. They never break character.

### 3.3 WhatsApp Coach (Spaced Repetition)
The WhatsApp bot bridges the gap between the App UI and the Practice Session. 

**Structure of a WhatsApp Challenge:**
1. **Context (Spanish/Spanglish):** Direct, no greetings. Focus on the task.
2. **The Prompt (English):** The exact phrase to repeat.
3. **The Feedback (Spanglish):** Quick, algorithmic, actionable.

**Examples of WhatsApp SR Interactions:**

*The Ask:*
> [MasteryTalk PRO] Recordatorio de pronunciación. 
> Tu punto débil de ayer fue la fluidez en compromisos de fechas.
> 
> Envía un audio diciendo: "We need to align on the deliverables before the end of the sprint."

*The Feedback (Good):*
> Score: 88/100. Pronunciación de 'deliverables' perfecta. Marcada como dominada. 
> Siguiente revisión en 3 días.

*The Feedback (Needs Work):*
> Score: 62/100. Cortaste la fluidez en 'align'. Sonó inseguro.
> En alta gerencia, la duda genera desconfianza.
> Inténtalo una vez más.
